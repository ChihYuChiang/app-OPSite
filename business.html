Business Project

Business Proposal, ​Talent OverFlow

2015  |  Group of 4
Entrepreneurial proposal for an on-line platform for start-up team formation, matching talents and start-ups by needed skills.
> Edraw / Trello
​> System Architecture / Strategic Planning / Proposal / Project Mgmt
Always aspiring to start a business of my own, I found that the most critical challenge for an early stage start-up is to gather around you the right talents, with proper skills, under a unified vision and goal. While the entire East Asia lacks a mature start-up community, such as that of Silicon Valley, this need itself became one of my entrepreneurial ideas.

In 2015, I put together a team, with colleagues from XPEC Entertainment, named NOICHI, and wrote this proposal for the platform, Talent OverFlow.

It is a web platform, establishing a community for talents in specific areas (in early phase, computer-based application related areas), on which the talents declare and develop specialties, and apply them to real projects. At the meantime, it creates an open and easily accessible talent database for talent-talent interaction. (eg. learning from each other or engaging different talents to proceed a project) Ultimately, it exercises the innovative energy from the talents, facilitating real projects and forming a positive cycle to create societal progression.

(The work was temporarily suspended, due to core members' career developments.)
Product Concept
Includes start-up vision, mission, value proposition, core team info, and product concept, demonstrated in specs and user stories.
Business Model
Includes target audience, the core business process, commercialization, and expected milestones, with a declaration of the core team's competitive edges.

Total Process Improvement and Business Plan, ​TutorABC

2009  |  Group of 7
Standard working process establishment of an on-line learning platform, plus its Business Plan for venture financing in Great China.
> Visio / Word / Powerpoint / SPSS
​>  Strategic Planning / Financial Forecasting / Proposal / Process Mgmt / Data Mining and Analysis
TutorABC is a language E-learning platform, selected as the 2012 Red Herring Top 100 Global, and now one of the major on-line learning providers in East Asia.
> Official Site

During the summer internship of my business study at the National Taiwan University, I worked for an emerging company, undertaking and developing a Total Process Improvement Project that reviewed and revised 30 key working processes across departments. It improved process efficiency, and provided faster customer service feedback, with more structured and careful follow-ups, which greatly reduced customer complaints.

Also, I devised a Corporate Business Plan for the company's fund raising from top ventures in Great China. It included a definition of company core competency, a business model, market and competition analysis, and a rough financial plan.
Work Flow Sample
​The team defined and renovated each working process in the company, and documented them in formal flow charts.

Annual Marketing Plan, ​3M Command

2008  |  Group of 5
Annual marketing plan for the Command product line in Taiwan. It integrates game elements with executable promotion campaigns.
> Word / Powerpoint
​> Strategic Planning / Marketing / Project Mgmt / Proposal / Data Analysis
One of a Business School student's special recreation is different kinds of case workshops and case competition. During my studies at National Taiwan University, I took part several times in these and worked out solutions to real business problems with respected enterprises partners, such as 3M and DIESEL.

The solution reports included strategy development, executable campaigns, and financial plans. These were my first opportunity to apply game ideas in a business context.

For the 3M marketing campaigns, we designed a finger-guessing game that integrated how to use the product by using game gestures. In addition, we designed a set of family figurines, according to Command Strip features. It was intended to create a family usage context that shortened the distance between the product and its target users.
