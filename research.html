The Essence of ​Game Experience

2014  |  Individual
Qualitative research. Defines game experience constituents, and clarifies differences between game and non-game experiences with gaming and psychology models.
> Edraw
​> Qualitative Research Method / Proposal
The Essence of Game Experience is a general framework, visualizing the relationship between gaming elements and satisfying game experiences.

After realizing the lack of systematic knowledge in game design, I conducted an independent literature review of relevant knowledge, exploring 20+ gaming models and psychology theories, combined with design and business expertise, piecing together the logic picture behind the game scenes.

In general, the game world is a combination of game components, mechanisms, narratives, and sensory features. With the support of technology, different styles of game worlds are materialized and attempt to satisfy players’ assortments of psychological needs, which probably are derived from people's various types of temperament.
​
If the attempts succeed, the fulfillment makes players have “fun” and, with good pacing, proper difficulty levels, and well-timed feedback and content release, creates the state of flow, which  amplifies the psychological satisfaction accrued during the game play.

Knowledge Transfer between Corporate Ventures and Their Parents

﻿2015  |  Individual
Quantitative research. Discusses corporate ventures' innovative performance from the perspective of Knowledge-based View and Resource Dependency.
> Stata / Word / Excel / Powerpoint
​> Quantitative Research Method / Proposal
Knowledge Transfer between Corporate Ventures and Their Parents was the research title of my Master's thesis in Business Administration at National Taiwan University.

It explored the issues of knowledge transfer between corporate ventures. With four decades of worldwide data from prominent business databases, SDC, Compustat, and United States Patent and Trademark Office Database, it statistically confirmed 5 hypotheses, including an inverted U-shaped relationship between parent institutes’ investment scale and corporate ventures’ innovative performance.

The research discovery was honored and chosen to be furthered by the National Science Council, the country’s highest science agency.

Feasibility Analysis of Margin Trading and Short Selling

2008  |  Individual
Qualitative research. Through literature review analyzes the policy feasibility of the Taiwanese security market's margin trading and short selling.
> Word
​> Qualitative Research Method / Proposal
Feasibility Analysis of Margin Trading and Short Selling was my first formal research work when I worked as part time policy researcher for Yuanta Financial Holdings, the largest securities house in Taiwan ( with a 12% market share in brokerage and 20% in margin financing in 2012).

The independent research reviewed current margin trading and the short selling policy, real practice cases, and experience referenced in other countries to form future policy suggestions.
